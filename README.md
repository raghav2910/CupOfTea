# CupOfTea
  Making a cup of tea.

## Ingredients used
   1. Water
   2. Milk
   3. Tea leaves 
   4. Sugar

### Steps 
   * boil water.
   * add tea leaves and boil it until the water becomes blackish.
   * then add milk and sugar to it according to your taste and health.
   * boil it until rises to some height .
   * then serve it in cups.
